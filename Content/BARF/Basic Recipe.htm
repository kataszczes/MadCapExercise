Basic Recipe

Here's a simple, basic recipe that you can tailor according to your needs. This recipe features chicken and duck, but you can you different meats.

The amounts will produce about 1 kg of finished food. Adjust accordingly to your dog's weight and activity level as well as the number of days that you want to prepare the food for.

Ingredients

  • 360g of duck breast without skin

  • 260g of chicken wings with bone and skin

  • 100g of chicken gizzards

  • 100g of chicken hearts

  • 100g of cooked sweet potato

  • 50g of beef kidney

  • 40g of duck liver

  • 2 eggs

  • 3g of brewer's yeast

  • 2ml of Omega-3 oil

  • 2ml of cod liver oil

  • 1g of algae

Preparation

  1. Weigh the meat, offal and veggies.

  2. Peel the veggies and cook until very soft. Strain and blend them.

  3. In the meantime, cut or grind the meat and offal.

  4. Portion the meals and distribute into containers.

  5. Measure and add the supplements.

  6. Put the eggs into 2 containers (you do not have to distribute them equally).
